Make-Ups

Rescheduling any lesson is possible, provided 3 days prior notice is given. A class can only be rescheduled into an appropriate time when the classroom is not full, and is also appropriate for the student. ​

For instance a High level student cannot make-up a lesson in a Low level classroom.
