Here at Goodwin English, our goal is to provide a fun atmosphere for people of all ages to learn. we also understand that it takes confidence and a drive to learn in order to overcome the difficulties of a second language. With our high focus on conversation, our goal is for every student to have the confidence to speak English with anyone!

Private Lessons

great for students who really benefit from 1 on 1 time and really need to improve their English skills. Convenient for students of any age.

Group Lessons

Small group lessons, perfect for siblings, friends, couples and students of similar skill level! Learn together in a personal environment designed just for the group.

Enjoy a variety of activities designed to help simulate real life activities such as crafting, games and cooking!
